# Fur & Feathers — Interactive Prezi Deck Plan

This deck is structured for a collaborative presentation (Ashutosh, Sandip, Nimesh, and you). Each slide includes a tight headline, 3–5 punchy bullets, and optional presenter notes.

## Slide 1 — Title / Opening
- Headline: “Fur & Feathers: Adopt Smarter, Care Deeper”
- Subhead: “AI-guided pet discovery, shelter workflows, and human-first support”
- Visual: Hero pet + human image, brand gradients; add team names

## Slide 2 — Table of Contents
- 1) Problem & Opportunity
- 2) Solution & Pillars
- 3) Product Walkthrough (features)

- 5) Ops & Performance

## Slide 3 — Problem & Opportunity
- Fragmented adoption journey; slow shelter response loops; poor fit risk
- Adopters lack transparent context (health, behavior, timelines)
- Shelters juggle messaging, status updates, and records across tools
- Opportunity: unified, guided experience with automation + empathy

## Slide 4 — Solution Snapshot (Pillars)
- Guided discovery: rich filters, curated cards, behavioral context
- Operational clarity: applications, dashboards, status and messaging in one lane
- AI assist: pet detection, Furly chat, nudges/reminders
- Trust & safety: privacy-first policies, transparent data practices

## Slide 5 — Product Overview (Home & Narrative)
- Hero banner with mission and primary CTA to browse pets
- Sections: Featured pets, How It Works, AI Matchmaking, Testimonials
- Theming: glassmorphism, branded gradients, responsive layout
- Smooth reveal animations on key homepage blocks

## Slide 6 — Discovery & Browsing
- Browse pets with filters (breed, age, location, personality cues)
- Featured pets grid and pet detail pages with media and owner actions
- Saved favorites for adopters; quick navigation from dashboard
- CTA pathways from homepage, cards, and dashboards

## Slide 7 — AI Pet Detection & History
- Pet detection tool with history view for previous scans
- Supports quick identification and re-engagement on past detections
- Clear navigation from Tools menu and dashboards

## Slide 8 — Furly Chat & Messaging
- Furly chat assistant entry point; async messaging between adopters and shelters
- Inbox/messages routes surfaced in nav and dashboards
- Typing indicators, status cues, and reminders to reply

## Slide 9 — Adoption Workflows
- Applications page (list, statuses); Adoption form flow; status visibility
- Journey updates on adopter dashboard; “Next steps” nudges
- Buttons link to applications, messages, and favorites directly

## Slide 10 — Pet Management (Shelter)
- Manage Pets and Add Pet flows for shelters; edit pets
- Pending actions: photo/medical updates, follow-ups
- Shelter dashboard quick actions: add pet, review applications, messages

## Slide 11 — Dashboards (Adopter / Shelter / Admin)
- Adopter: hero metrics, journey status, quick links (pets, favorites, messages)
- Shelter: metrics (active pets, applications, adoption velocity), finance snapshots
- Admin: approvals, user and pet stats (mock fallback), monitoring views
- Interactive tablists now link to actual routes; responsive layout tweaks

## Slide 12 — Privacy & Terms
- Dedicated Privacy and Terms pages with plain-language summaries
- Controls: data export, deletion, email preferences
- Security: encryption in transit/at rest, incident response targets
- Retention and limited third-party usage are documented

## Slide 13 — UX & Brand System
- Glass surfaces, pill buttons, badges, cards; consistent container widths
- Responsive grids and mobile-friendly tablists/pills
- Reveal/fade animations on homepage with reduced-motion support
- CTA banner boxed to container (no edge bleed) when used elsewhere

## Slide 14 — Performance & Ops
- Built with Vite + React 18; chunk-size warning noted (~920 kB main bundle)
- Opportunities: dynamic imports/manualChunks for route-level code splitting
- Tailwind utilities + custom CSS; lucide-react icons; framer-motion in playground
- Jest + Testing Library scaffolded; CI build logs available

## Slide 15 — Demo Flow (Extra Slide #1)
- 1) Home hero → Browse pets
- 2) Pet detail → Apply → Messages
- 3) Adopter dashboard → Quick links (favorites/messages/applications)
- 4) Shelter dashboard → Add pet → Manage adoptions → Messages
- 5) Privacy/Terms for trust signal

## Slide 16 — Impact & Outcomes (Extra Slide #2)
- Faster placement: synced availability + guided applications
- Reduced back-and-forth: consolidated messaging and status views
- Better fit: behavioral context + AI assist → fewer returns
- Confidence: transparent policies and consistent UI

## Slide 17 — Roadmap (Extra Slide #3)

- Deeper analytics (engagement per feature, funnel drop-offs)
- Push/web notifications for status changes and meeting reminders
- Localization and accessibility pass for WCAG 2.1 AA
- Optional: Adoption fee payments and transport scheduling
