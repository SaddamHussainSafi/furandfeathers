### Slide 3: Introduction
Title: Connecting Hearts & Paws

Content:
   Mission: To bridge the gap between homeless pets and loving families through technology.
   Vision: A world where every pet finds a forever home efficiently and transparently.
   Overview: "Fur and Feathers" is a full-stack web application that modernizes the traditional adoption process.
   Key Differentiator: Unlike static listing sites, we use AI to actively match pets with suitable owners and automate shelter operations.

Speaker Notes: Introduce the team and the core passion behind the project. Emphasize that this is not just a website, but a platform.

---

### Slide 4: The Problem
Title: Challenges in Traditional Adoption

Content:
   Fragmented Systems: Adopters have to visit multiple shelter websites or physical locations to find a pet.
   Information Overload: Difficulty in finding specific breeds or pet types suitable for a user's lifestyle among thousands of listings.
   Manual Processes: Shelters are burdened with paper applications, manual tracking, and phone calls.
   Lack of Guidance: First-time adopters often lack immediate support or answers to their questions, leading to lower adoption rates.

---

### Slide 5: The Solution
Title: A Unified, Intelligent Ecosystem

Content:
   Centralized Hub: One platform connecting multiple shelters with thousands of potential adopters.
   Role-Based Access: Tailored experiences for Adopters, Shelters, Admins, and Super Admins.
   AI Integration: Leveraging Google Gemini and advanced algorithms to automate tasks (image recognition, chat support).
   Real-Time Interaction: Instant updates on applications, direct messaging, and live notifications.

---

### Slide 6: Core Architecture & Tech Stack
Title: Built on Modern Technologies

Content:
   Frontend: 
       React (Vite): For a lightning-fast, responsive user interface.
       Tailwind CSS: For modern, consistent styling.
       Framer Motion: For smooth, interactive animations.
   Backend:
       Spring Boot (Java 17): Robust, scalable REST API.
       Spring Security: Enterprise-grade authentication and authorization.
       JPA / Hibernate: Efficient ORM for database interactions.
   Database:
       MySQL: Reliable relational data storage for users, pets, and applications.
   AI Services:
       Google Gemini 2.5 Flash: Powering our intelligent features (Chat & Vision).

### Slide 7: Feature - Smart Authentication
Title: Secure & Seamless Access

Content:
   Multi-Role System: 
       Adopters: Browse, apply, chat.
       Shelters: Manage inventory, review applications.
       Admins: Approve shelters, oversee platform.
       Super Admin: Full system control.
   Security First: 
       JWT (JSON Web Tokens): Stateless, secure session management.
       BCrypt Hashing: Secure password storage.
   Ease of Use: 
       Google OAuth: One-click registration and login.
       Role-Based Redirection: Automatically routes users to their specific dashboard upon login.

### Slide 10: Feature - AI Companion "Furly"
Title: Meet Furly: Your AI Adoption Assistant

Content:
   24/7 Support: An intelligent chatbot available anytime to answer user queries.
   Matchmaking Mode: 
       Asks 5 key questions (Home environment, Activity level, Household members, Experience, Traits).
       Uses AI to recommend the perfect pet based on answers.
   Context-Aware: Understands user intent to provide relevant advice on pet care and adoption procedures.
   Conversation History: Stores chat sessions in the database for continuity.

---

### Slide 11: Feature - AI Pet Detection
Title: Smart Pet Recognition

Content:
   Automated Cataloging: Shelters can upload a photo, and our AI analyzes it instantly.
   Deep Analysis: Powered by Google Gemini 2.5 Flash.
       Species & Breed: Identifies probable breed mix.
       Age Estimation: Guesses age based on visual features.
       Emotional State: Detects if the pet looks happy, scared, or calm.
       Physical Traits: Notes fur color, length, and size.
   Efficiency: Drastically reduces the time needed to create detailed pet listings.
